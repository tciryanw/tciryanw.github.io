This is a client-side test application for remake software inside a barcrest hardware slot. View pic of hardware slotmachine.
You get reset to 'start' position if you hit a regular (empty) tile, any other tile you have choice to collect (if you have inplay balance) or continue.
Start and continue button cost 1 credit from bank. Ladders will place position to somewhere in field.
The single reel can go from 1 to 12. Additionally there is scatter symbol.
